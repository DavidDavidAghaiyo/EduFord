Our Facilities

EduFord is committed to providing a world-class learning environment through state-of-the-art facilities designed to enhance both academic and personal growth. Our campus features modern, well-equipped classrooms, advanced laboratories, and a comprehensive library stocked with physical and digital resources to support a wide range of studies. We prioritize technology-driven education, offering high-speed internet, smart learning tools, and innovative research spaces. For extracurricular engagement, we provide sports complexes, cultural activity centers, and wellness facilities to ensure a holistic experience. Additionally, our campus is designed for inclusivity and sustainability, with comfortable student accommodations, dining areas, and eco-friendly practices. At Edu Ford, we strive to create an environment where students can excel academically, grow personally, and prepare for a successful future.

World Class Library

EduFord's world-class library is a hub of knowledge and innovation, offering an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources across various disciplines. Equipped with modern technology, comfortable study spaces, and dedicated research zones, our library provides an ideal environment for learning and exploration. With expert support staff and 24/7 access to online materials, we ensure students and faculty have everything they need to excel in their academic pursuits.

Largest Sports Centre

EduFord's sports center is a vibrant space designed to promote fitness, teamwork, and health. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities
for indoor and outdoor games, it offers a variety of sports like basketball, football, badminton, and more. With professional coaching and ample recreational areas, our center encourages students to stay active, develop skills, and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Diverse Cafeteria

EduFord's cafeteria offers a diverse menu to cater to every taste, featuring nutritious, freshly prepared meals and snacks from around the world. With a welcoming ambiance and hygienic practices, it’s the perfect spot for students to relax, recharge, and connect over delicious food
